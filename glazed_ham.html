Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 2-3 hours Servings: 10-12 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8-10 lb) bone-in ham
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • Whole cloves for studding

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325¬∞F (165¬∞C)
  2. Score ham in diamond pattern, stud with cloves
  3. Place ham in roasting pan with rack
  4. Mix honey, butter, brown sugar, mustard, and spices
  5. Brush ham with glaze mixture
  6. Bake for 2-3 hours, basting every 30 minutes
  7. Let rest 20 minutes before serving
