Rich and Creamy Hot Chocolate

Prep Time: 10 minutes Servings: 2 cups

Is there anything cozier on a chilly winter day than a steaming cup of homemade hot chocolate? This recipe is rich, creamy, and filled with the comforting chocolatey goodness perfect for any snowy evening. Curl up with a blanket, this drink, and enjoy the holiday vibe!

Ingredients:

  • Two cups whole milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream, marshmallows, or cinnamon stick for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together milk, cocoa powder, and sugar over medium heat. Continue to stir until the mixture is heated through.
  2. Add the chocolate chips and stir until melted, creating a luscious, creamy base.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Pour into mugs. Top with whipped cream, marshmallows, or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
